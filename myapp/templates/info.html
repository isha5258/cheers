Welcome to Cheers!

With the vastly growing micro brewery industry and endlessly rotating beers on tap at your favorite breweries, keeping track of what you like or even what you had two weeks ago can be tough. Allow Cheers to keep track of your beer posts. Cheers will let you track your posts and view posts from other users as well.

You will notice while posting a new beer that there is not a rating system. This was intentional. Rating can be very subjective, and while one type of beer may not be to someones liking such as: ipas, stouts, etc, we don't want that to effect the overall rating of the beer. Instead! we opted for just a comments section, and users encourage all users to utilize to describe what they liked or disliked about the beer.

Begin tracking your beers today!
